We have just returned from a two week stay in the beautiful Krabi region of Thailand. During our stay we island hopped and went elephant trekking. What made our holiday special was the rich cultural experience provided by the locals we met. We were given the opportunity to "live/eat like a local" and discover the hidden gems of the Andaman Sea.

Rebecca, Robert and Alonzo, Thailand 2015

I am married with two young boys so this year my husband and I decided to go on a camping holiday to Huttopia in Versaills and we had a pleasant experience. The Huttopia Versailles is a relaxing haven in the forest near Versailles and only 20mins from where we live, Paris.

Chantale, Summer 2016

My husband and I went to Rome on a city break and where overwhelmed by its beauty. We visited the classical ruins and early Christian places of worship that stood next to the Renaissance palazzos and Baroque fountains. Strolling through the city we stumbled upon quirky shops embracing the buzzing scene. The city's mild Mediterranean climate was also refreshing for us, but for me the main draw to Rome was the pulsating energy of a place which lives life as a form of theatre. Mrs McAnea, Rome 2013.
